THE SPIRIT UNCONQUERED

VIREN

Geological phenomena, biological evolution, climate diversity, karma, bad omens, spirits, and the supernatural are all intertwined in the age of Pangaea, a supercontinent where all life forms once thrived. However, there was an era when beasts, spirits, and monsters of eldritch origin unleashed their fury upon mortals. Among them rose Viren, a legendary warrior, who vanquished beasts and saved humanity.

However, even Viren met their equal in a cataclysmic clash between a beast from the hellish Manticore and an ancient sabretooth. The battle shook the very earth, causing islands to tear apart and families to separate, shattering Pangaea. Viren and the beast called a truce, swore an oath to rebuild what they had destroyed.

From dusk to dawn, they toiled side by side, guiding humanity with strength bound to purpose. Our hero, Viren endures not for glory alone but to lead and protect when the world needs strength most. SOLEN

After the Great Divide, nations sought to restore balance and create communities, states, political authority, innovation, and development. They sought a guiding hand to lead humanity towards a brighter tomorrow. From the Kingdom Solidaria came Solen, a prodigy gifted in peace and diplomacy. They dreamed to weave bonds among strangers and forge unity out of division.

However, Solen's vision faltered when the Sky and Sea, two primordial forces, clashed in fury. Tempests howled and floods consumed the lands, threatening to unmake what little order remained. But Solen stood between them, pleading for a world where all could coexist in harmony. Through trials of storm and tide, came the Sea of Mirrors, a vast veil shielding Solen's utopia from malevolent eyes.

In gratitude, the Sky and Sea bestowed upon Solen two gifts: a royal scepter forged from the thunder and the depths of the abyss, symbolizing their dominion, and a wondrous companion born of qilin's grace and the narwhal's might. With the scepter and a loyal companion, Solen became the eternal Shepherd of Sky and Sea, guiding humanity towards unity beneath the endless horizon.

LUXEN

The Dark Ages were a time of loss and destruction, marked by the hunt for the Guardian of Knowledge, a being who bestowed wisdom, invention, and the means for human flourishing. However, greed corrupted humankind, leading to its disappearance and the world's decline. In this despair, an albino scholar, Luxen, lived in solitude, collecting knowledge from ruined monasteries and abandoned libraries. They pored over forbidden scrolls and forged crude medicines in secret. However, they found their true teacher in nature, the Guardian of Knowledge, who was wounded and hunted. Luxen chose compassion, tending to its wounds, hiding it, and nursing it back to health. The Guardian revealed hidden truths, teaching Luxen the cycles of growth and renewal that bind all life.

Their journeys and compassion led to the birth of a hybrid creature, the Scholar of the Sun and Moon. They reformed the world, restoring balance and gifting wisdom where ignorance had taken root. During a great eclipse, the Sun and Moon descended, blessing Luxen with brilliance and eternal wisdom. From then on, Luxen became the Scholar of the Sun and Moon, a beacon of light in humanity's darkest hours, proving that compassion and perseverance could awaken knowledge even when the world had already abandoned it.
